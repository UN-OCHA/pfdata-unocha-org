Covid - 19 Allocations
CERF and CBPF pooled funds have allocated a combined total of US$222 million to respond to the COVID-19 pandemic. To date, the funds have supported a broad range of humanitarian partners to launch time-critical projects in over 43 countries. Additional opportunities to support partners responding to the Global COVID-19 HRP continue to be identified.
